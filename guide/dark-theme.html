# Dark Theme

# How it's works ?

To enable the dark you have just to add dark class to the <html /> of your page

<!-- Dark mode not enabled -->
<html>
<body>
  <!-- Will be white -->
  <div id="app">
    <!-- ... -->
  </div>
</body>
</html>

<!-- Dark mode enabled -->
<html class="dark">
<body>
  <!-- Will be black -->
  <div id="app">
    <!-- ... -->
  </div>
</body>
</html>

# How to manage theme ?

Use the theme-handler composable: theme-handler
